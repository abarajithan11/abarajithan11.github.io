Linny (My Linh Hong - the girl who took me around Saigon, Vietnam)'s friend arrived in Brisbane for a business visit. Linny asked me to take care of her. I took her around Gold Coast on a bicycle.

We biked about 40 km around the rich people's canals and among waves on the beach in Surfer's paradise, played with kangaroos and emus in Currumbin, tasted a cheesecake, and slow-cooked pork bun in Miami...

## Canals

![]({{ site.content_base_url }}/images/2022/01/IMG_20180825_140243.jpg)

Rich neighborhoods of gold coasts have this intricate canal system. Each house has a driveway (for cars) and a canal (for boats). We cycled through them.
