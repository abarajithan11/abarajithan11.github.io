Site: [kavigai.org/](http://kavigai.org/)

**My Responsibilities** as a co-founder and Director of External Relations during 2017-2018 were:

- Monitoring the growth of the organization
- Finding sponsorship, partnerships
- Building a brand for the non-profit foundation
- Dealing with university administration and legal bodies
