Namunukula is a mountain range on the eastern side of the central hills of Sri Lanka, with a forest of turpentine trees. It is reachable from Badulla. This post has several gorgeous photos, many shot by my good friend [Chinthana](https://web.facebook.com/c4ms95), as watermarked.

![]({{ site.content_base_url }}/images/2022/01/CHW_0024.jpg)

![]({{ site.content_base_url }}/images/2022/01/CHW_0023.jpg)

![]({{ site.content_base_url }}/images/2022/01/CHW_0137.jpg)![]({{ site.content_base_url }}/images/2022/01/CHW_0010-1.jpg)

The famed 9-arch bridge. A British architectural marvel in Sri Lanka

We took the popular Colombo - Badulla night mail train and reached there by morning. We had breakfast and hiked through the tea plantations to reach the turpentine forest.
