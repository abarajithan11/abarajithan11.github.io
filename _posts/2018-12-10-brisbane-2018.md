Brisbane is a vibrant city full of energy. The best way to explore Brisbane would be on a bicycle. Bicycle paths are designed to provide the most beautiful views. There is a 'City Cycle' system: one can use a Translink (bus/train) card to travel around the city on a bicycle. Mt. Coot-Tha is a nearby suburb with a beautiful botanical garden and a large telescope. The international food culture of Brisbane is fabulous as well.

## Food

![]({{ site.content_base_url }}/images/2022/01/IMG_20180909_184015.jpg)
