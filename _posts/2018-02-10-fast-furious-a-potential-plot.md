Just realiz*ed that riding a sports car out of earth is the perfect plot for the Fast and Furious 9!*

## Plot

Prequel (1977): Some billionaire created the coolest and the most expensive vintage supercar. Some Chinese mafia have stolen the key. The group's fathers chase them through the streets of Florida. Finally, they put the nitro booster and accidentally fly over the Cape Canaveral launchpad of NASA.

They get into fistfight while flying over the rocket. The key slips from their hand and falls into the rocket which is about to launch, into Voyager 2. And the key is lost forever...

Sequel (2020): ...until it is not. These buggers want to bring back the key and restore their fathers' glory. They take a Tesla roadster on a mission, chasing voyager 2 out of the solar system, to bring back the key for the vintage supercar and save the planet!
